# World Map

## Key Locations in Midgaard

### City Center
- **Market Square** - Central hub with statue, connects to temple, common square, main street
- **Main Street** - Runs east-west through city
- **Temple Square** - North of market square

### Guilds
- **Guild of Swordsmen** - Main street (east section), south of weapon shop
  - Entrance Hall
  - Bar of Swordsmen (has bulletin board)
  - **Tournament and Practice Yard** - WHERE YOU PRACTICE SKILLS
    - Contains guildmaster
    - Has well leading down (d command)
  
- **Clerics' Guild** - West of temple square
  - Entrance with knight templar guard

- **Guild of Magic Users** - West side of city

### Shops & Businesses
- **Bakery** - North of main street (starting location)
- **Armory** - South of main street (west section)
- **Weapon Shop** - North of main street (east section)
- **General Store** - North of main street (center)
- **Pet Shop** - South of main street (center)
- **Magic Shop** - North of west end

### Inns & Taverns
- **Grubby Inn** - South of poor alley
- **Grunting Boar Inn** - East of temple square

### Other
- **Temple of Midgaard** - North of temple square
  - Reading Room (had teleporter device)
  - Donation room
- **Poor Alley** - West of common square
- **Common Square** - South of market square
- **Dark Alley** - East of common square

## NPCs
- Guildmaster (Guild of Swordsmen practice yard)
- Knight (guarding Swordsmen guild entrance)
- Waiter (Bar of Swordsmen)
- Cityguards (various locations)
- Peacekeeper (various locations)
- Beastly fido (garbage areas)

## Travel Notes
